Organizer Platform

Run world-class competitions — effortlessly

From setup to awards, Compify gives organizers one powerful platform to configure, recruit, manage, and analyze every aspect of their competition.

Step 1

Configure your competition from scratch

Set up every detail before day one: general info, evaluation sessions, scoring criteria, and a real-time dashboard to monitor it all. One central hub replaces scattered spreadsheets.

📊 Dashboard

A bird's-eye view of your competition: key metrics, registration trends, and session status — all in one glance.

📋 Details

Set competition name, dates, location and execution type. One central place instead of scattered spreadsheets.

🗓 Sessions

Create parallel evaluation sessions by product type (e.g. Nihonshu, Shochu, Design), each with its own categories.

✏️ Evaluation Forms

Standard scoring criteria (Sweetness, Acidity, Body…) plus custom questions. Every judge evaluates on the same scale.

Step 2

Recruit judges, stewards & producers

Gather all participants through structured application and registration flows. Judges apply, producers register, stewards are coordinated — no one falls through the cracks.

📝 Candidacy

Build application forms for judges and stewards. Review, approve or reject every candidacy from a single inbox.

🎟 Registration

Dedicated signup flow for producers entering their products. Separate from jury applications for clarity.

👨‍⚖️ Jury

Full judge & steward directory: profiles, qualifications, session assignments. Ensures expertise and impartiality.

Step 3

Manage competition day like a pro

Products, companies, table assignments and live control. The operational heart of competition day — every person and bottle knows exactly where to go.

🏢 Entries

Complete registry of participating companies and products: name, category, origin, assigned session. The core of the competition.

🎛 Control Room

Real-time dashboard: start/pause sessions, monitor votes, track progress across all categories at a glance.

🪑 Tables

Create evaluation tables and assign products and judges. Every person and bottle knows exactly where to go.

Step 4

Analyze results & close the business loop

Turn raw data into professional reports, rankings and certificates. Configure awards, manage billing, and keep everyone informed with automated notifications.

📈 Reports & Statistics

Auto-generated product reports with radar charts, KPI dashboards, geographic distribution. Export as PDF or Excel.

💰 Billing

Configure participation fees, early-bird rates, volume discounts. Collect invoice data automatically — no email back-and-forth.

🏆 Categories & Awards

Define award tiers (Gold, Silver, Bronze), set thresholds, generate winner certificates. The culmination of every competition.

🔔 Notifications

Automated emails and alerts for every milestone: registration confirmed, session starting, results published. Everyone stays informed.
